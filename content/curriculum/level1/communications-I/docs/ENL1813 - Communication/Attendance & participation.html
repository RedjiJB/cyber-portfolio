Use of generative AI  (GenAI) tools is permitted in this course. 

Ethical and effective GenAI use is part of course assessment and is outlined in assignment instructions.

Inputs (prompts) should be provided when requested, and generated outputs will be assessed as prescribed in the Brightspace rubrics/evaluations.

Students are responsible for the content they submit, whether produced solely by an individual or in conjunction with a technology, such as a generative AI.
